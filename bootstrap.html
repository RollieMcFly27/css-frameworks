Technology: Always Forward?

by Julian Mendiola on 6/21/2023

Technology is a force that continues to move forward without stopping, but when we think to upgrade them, what becomes of our faithful machines destined for the scrapyard? What can we do with them now that something better has come along? The answer may be easier than you realize.

Pass It On!

by Julian Mendiola on 6/21/2023

You got your first computer, that is great! But your older model can still be of some use. Somewhere out there is a family or a friend who wishes to have a computer, but cannot afford one. Your old model may not be top of the line, but it can still be useful. Don't throw away, give away your old technology so it can still be used!
